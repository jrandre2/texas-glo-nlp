How to use

  • Dashboards / maps: open the .html links in your browser.
  • Tables: open .csv in Excel/Sheets. Use filters to explore.
  • Master database: data/glo_reports.db can be opened with “DB Browser for SQLite”.
  • Interpretation: “money mentions” are extracted from narrative text and are not official accounting totals.
